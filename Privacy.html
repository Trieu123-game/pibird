Privacy Policy

Welcome to Flappi Sky Master. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all Pioneers on their journey "GO TO THE MOON".

1. Information We Collect

To provide the gaming experience and global rankings, we may collect the following information via Pi Network API:

  • Pi Username: To display on the Global Leaderboard.
  • High Score: To track your best performance.
  • Wallet Address: Only if required for future ecosystem rewards or transactions.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for:

  • Maintaining and updating the global player rankings.
  • Authenticating users within the Pi Network ecosystem.
  • Improving game physics and audio performance.

3. Data Security

We store score data locally on your device (LocalStorage) and on our secure servers. We never ask for your wallet Passphrase.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to any third parties outside the necessary scope of the Pi Network operations.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your score data or username from our records at any time by contacting the administrator.
